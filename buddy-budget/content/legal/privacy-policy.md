## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 3.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and understand how the Service is used.

### 3.2 Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

**Strictly Necessary Cookies** (Always Active)
- Required for the Service to function properly
- Enable core functionality such as authentication, theme preferences, and session management
- Cannot be disabled without affecting Service functionality

**Functionality Cookies** (Optional)
- Enable enhanced features and personalization
- Remember your preferences and settings
- May be set by us or third-party providers

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional)
- Help us understand how visitors interact with the Service
- Collect and report information anonymously
- Used to improve Service performance and user experience

**Marketing/Advertisement Cookies** (Optional)
- May be used to deliver relevant advertisements
- Track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns
- Limit the number of times you see an advertisement

### 3.3 Managing Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent banner. You can:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences by cookie category

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling necessary cookies may affect Service functionality.

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use (e.g., analytics providers). These cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy.

## 4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

### 4.1 Data Storage

- Your financial data and account information are stored securely on our servers
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure protocols, and access controls
- Financial data you input is stored in a structured format for processing and visualization

### 4.2 Data Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest

- **Regular Security Audits**: Ongoing monitoring and security assessments

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
